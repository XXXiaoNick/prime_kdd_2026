# Figures

Curated figures from the PRIME paper, in both **PNG** (preview) and **PDF** (vector)
formats. All figures are generated by [`prime/visualization.py`](../prime/visualization.py)
from experiment outputs. They are aggregate analyses and contain **no raw data**.

## Main paper figures

| Paper figure | File(s) | Content |
|:---:|---|---|
| **Fig. 1** | `fig1_energy_landscape_theoretical` | Theoretical energy landscape in (E<sub>bull</sub>, E<sub>bear</sub>) space with learned (α, β, γ); top-ranked stocks cluster in the low-energy basin. |
| **Fig. 3a** | `fig3a_game_phase_space_csi500` | Game phase space — equilibrium of selected stocks (CSI 500). |
| **Fig. 3b** | `fig3b_game_phase_space_sp500` | Game phase space (S&P 500). |
| **Fig. 3c** | `fig3c_guardian_distribution_kde` | Risk-Guardian score KDE — safe vs. crash separation. |
| **Fig. 3d** | `fig3d_guardian_distribution_boxplot` | Risk-Guardian discriminability (boxplot). |
| **Fig. 4a** | `fig4a_energy_landscape_empirical` | Empirical energy landscape projected on PC1–PC2 (train vs. test trajectories). |
| **Fig. 4b** | `fig4b_correlation_energy_components` | Spearman correlation among energy components, parameters, and returns. |
| **Fig. 5** | `fig5_energy_landscape_{bull,neutral,bear}` | Regime-dependent energy landscapes (Total Energy vs. Momentum & Resistance) with steepest-descent paths. |
| **Fig. 6** | `fig6_correlation_{bull,neutral,bear}`, `fig6_correlation_market_states` | Regime-dependent energy correlations across bull / neutral / bear states. |
| **Fig. 7a** | `fig7a_training_loss_curve` | Training-loss convergence across 30 epochs for all methods. |
| **Fig. 7b** | `fig7b_efficiency_gpu_memory` | GPU memory consumption during training. |
| **Fig. 7c** | `fig7c_efficiency_vs_performance` | Efficiency–performance trade-off (model size vs. risk-adjusted return). |

> **Fig. 2** (overall architecture of PRIME) is a hand-designed schematic rather than
> a generated plot, so it is not reproduced here; see the paper for the diagram.

## Supplementary figures (`supplementary/`)

- **Ablation** — `ablation_comparison_{annual_return,sharpe_ratio,test_ic}`,
  `ablation_contribution`, `ablation_heatmap`
- **Robustness** — `robustness_{noise,topk,lr,epochs,summary}`

  `sr_convergence_curve`

## Regenerating

Figures are produced as a side effect of training / experiment runs and the
visualization utilities. For example:

```bash
python prime/main.py --mode train --market_profile csi500 --asset_type stock --fast
# → figures and diagnostics under outputs/figures/ , outputs/correlation/ , outputs/landscape/ , outputs/efficiency/
```
